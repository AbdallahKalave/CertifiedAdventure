Experience Overview

The absolute packed tour with thrills, adrenaline, entertainment and barbecue dinner. Get Set and go straight into the highway as we head towards the amazing red sand dunes of Arabia. Start the tour with some great skills of driving skills by our safari marshals who cut through the steep sand dunes to make it a fun-filled drive to enjoy.


Stop for pictures and enjoy the sunset before heading to our camp, where you will be welcomed with Luqaimat, Arabic coffee, Tea and Dates. Enjoy a camel ride, try the hubbly bubbly (shisha) and ask our henna designer to help you with a tattoo.


Enjoy the Falafel, Veg-sambousek and Chicken-sambousek as you settle down to enjoy the rest of the evening, which starts with a sizzling belly dance performance, followed by a lavish buffet with freshly made barbecue. The evening ends with a colourful tanoura dance performer in a Sufi dance style and a stunning fire show before we head back home with unforgettable moments and memories to cherish all life long. A perfect evening with a perfect tour booked with Certified Adventure.

Itinerary

Your tour begins with an unforgettable roller coaster drive in the desert skimming over the magnificent Sand Dunes. Amidst all the fun and action, we stop for pictures. After which we proceed towards our camp.A traditional welcome awaits our guests with Arabic coffee, and tea and dates while the charcoal fires and the traditional oven is lit in preparation for our evening dinner. Relax in our cozy outdoor lounge areas, furnished with plush sofas and cushions. Enjoy the tranquility of the desert while sipping on refreshing beverages. Get a henna tattoo or experience an unforgettable camel ride while we wait for the highlight of the evening which includes a Tannoura dance show , a Fire show and an exotic belly performance to the rhythmic beat of Arabia. Our evening ends with a delicious four course barbeque meal with a variety of meats and salads served beneath the starlit skies. After dinner we drive back to the back to the city.

Optional :-ATV /Quad bikes / Dune buggy at extra cost payable on spot.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get the refund?

We understand that plans change. You can cancel your reservation up to 48 hours before your experience for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours of your experience will be subject to a 50% cancellation fee.

Can I change the travel date?

Our booking policy allows for modifications up to 48 hours before your scheduled experience. Please contact us at +971 50 242 4760 or support@certifiedadventuretourism.com to discuss any changes you’d like to make to your reservation.

Do you offer insurance coverage for my adventure?

We prioritize your safety and enjoyment during your experience. While we don’t offer comprehensive travel insurance, we do provide coverage for specific aspects of your adventure:

  • Transportation and pick-up/drop-off: Your transportation to and from the activity is covered in case of unforeseen circumstances.
  • Dune bashing: Coverage is included for any incidents that might occur during the dune bashing portion of your experience.
  • On-site camp: While you’re at the designated camp for your chosen activity, you’re covered for any insured events.

It’s important to note that this coverage is limited to the mentioned scenarios. For any additional protection, we recommend considering personal travel insurance.
