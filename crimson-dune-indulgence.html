Experience Overview

Get the best when you book the Desert Safari with us. Sit back and enjoy the thrills, frills and adrenaline when we drive through the raw untamed desert in our four-wheel drives by our professional and well-trained safari marshals.

As the sunsets we drive ahead to our local Bedouin campsite where you can enjoy riding the camel and get yourself a henna tattoo as we set in for the evening ahead with some lively performance by the Sufi Master, the fire blower and the belly dancer along with lip-smacking dinner accompanied by freshly prepared barbecues.

Itinerary

Pickup & Departure
We pick you up from your hotel or preferred location in a professional 4x4 Land Cruiser. The journey to the Lahbab desert takes approximately 45-60 minutes.
Dune Bashing & Sandboarding
Experience 30 minutes of heart-pounding dune bashing across the crimson sands. We stop at the highest dunes for photo opportunities and sandboarding sessions.
Sunset Photos & Camel Ride
Capture the magical desert sunset before heading to the Bedouin-style camp for a traditional camel ride and welcoming refreshments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get the refund?

We understand that plans change. You can cancel your reservation up to 48 hours before your experience for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours of your experience will be subject to a 50% cancellation fee.

Can I change the travel date?

Our booking policy allows for modifications up to 48 hours before your scheduled experience. Please contact us to discuss any changes you’d like to make.

Do you offer insurance coverage for my adventure?

We prioritize your safety. While we don’t offer comprehensive travel insurance, we provide coverage for transportation, dune bashing incidents, and on-site camp events. For additional protection, we recommend personal travel insurance.
