Premium Desert Safari

Crimson Dune Indulgence: BBQ & Entertainment

Experience Overview

Get the best when you book the Desert Safari with us. Sit back and enjoy the thrills, frills and adrenaline when we drive through the raw untamed desert in our four-wheel drives by our professional and well-trained safari marshals. Click your memories when we stop for photo ops in the highest dune during the drive and also enjoy sand boarding at the same time.

As the sunsets we drive ahead to our local Bedouin campsite where you can enjoy riding the camel and get yourself a henna tattoo as we set in for the evening ahead with some lively performance by the Sufi Master – the Tanoura performer, the showstopper – The fire blower and the mover and shaker – The Belly Dancer along with some lip-smacking dinner accompanied by freshly prepared barbecues along with the soothing Arabic music plays in the background.

Try the local hubbly bubbly (Shisha) which is offered with different flavors to match your taste buds, as we prepare to leave you can get your name embossed in a sand bottle by the sand artist available on site at an extra cost before we head back to the city to drop you off with lots of memories to cherish with a lifetime.

  • Any accidents or damages from the activity will be the sole responsibility of the rider and passenger

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get the refund?

We understand that plans change. You can cancel your reservation up to 48 hours before your experience for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours of your experience will be subject to a 50% cancellation fee.

Can I change the travel date?

Our booking policy allows for modifications up to 48 hours before your scheduled experience. Please contact us at +971 50 242 4760 or support@certifiedadventuretourism.com to discuss any changes you’d like to make to your reservation.

Do you offer insurance coverage for my adventure?

We prioritize your safety and enjoyment during your experience. While we don’t offer comprehensive travel insurance, we do provide coverage for specific aspects of your adventure:

  • Transportation and pick-up/drop-off: Your transportation to and from the activity is covered in case of unforeseen circumstances.
  • Dune bashing: Coverage is included for any incidents that might occur during the dune bashing portion of your experience.
  • On-site camp: While you’re at the designated camp for your chosen activity, you’re covered for any insured events.

It’s important to note that this coverage is limited to the mentioned scenarios. For any additional protection, we recommend considering personal travel insurance.
