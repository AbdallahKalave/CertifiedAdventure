Itinerary

Classic Desert safari with Quad bike experience is a perfect combination booked. Get to try your hand on riding the Quad bike amidst the wild sands of the desert and this makes its an exciting adventure to remember for a life time. A prefect excursion where in you get to ride the quad bike for a hand on experience by yourself before you are taken on a roller coaster drive through the sand dunes by our professional marshal.

This package also includes photo opportunities along with sand boarding before we head out to the local Bedouin village to spend the rest of the evening. As you arrive you can help yourself with a camel ride experience as well get a tattoo from our henna designer. You can taste the Arabic coffee along with some dates before you get cosy on the low laid cushions in a majlis style to give you the Arabic feeling.

The evening ahead has some great shows like the Fire blower, the Tanura performer and last but not the least the belly dancer to moves and shakes along with the typical Arabic music to match up to with. The evening ends with some Arabic food complimented with live fresh barbeque before we head back home with life time moments to be remembered all life time.

My discount code is not working, what do I do?

We understand the frustration of a discount code not working! Here are a few things to check:

  • Double-check the code: Ensure you've entered the code exactly as displayed, including any uppercase or lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Minimum spend requirement: Some discount codes have a minimum amount you need to spend for them to apply. Review the code details to see if this applies.
  • Expiration date: Make sure the discount code is still valid. Expired codes won't work.
  • Applicable plan: Verify if the code is valid for the specific plan you're trying to book. Some codes might be limited to certain experiences.

If you've checked all these points and the code still doesn't work, feel free to contact us at +971 50 242 4760 or support@certifiedadventuretourism.com. We'll be happy to help!

Do you offer insurance coverage for my adventure?

We prioritize your safety and enjoyment during your experience. While we don't offer comprehensive travel insurance, we do provide coverage for specific aspects of your adventure:

  • Transportation and pick-up/drop-off: Your transportation to and from the activity is covered in case of unforeseen circumstances.
  • Dune bashing: Coverage is included for any incidents that might occur during the dune bashing portion of your experience.
  • On-site camp: While you're at the designated camp for your chosen activity, you're covered for any insured events.

It's important to note that this coverage is limited to the mentioned scenarios. For any additional protection, we recommend considering personal travel insurance.
