Experience Overview

Your tour begins with an unforgettable roller coaster drive in the desert, skimming over the magnificent Sand Dunes. Amidst all the fun and action, we stop for pictures as we head towards our traditional Bedouin campsite.

A traditional welcome awaits our guests with Arabic coffee, tea, and dates while the charcoal fires and traditional oven are lit in preparation for our evening dinner. Relax in our cozy outdoor lounge areas, furnished with plush sofas and cushions, as you soak in the desert tranquility.

Tour Itinerary

1. Pickup & Dune Bashing

Adventure starts with a professional pickup from your doorstep followed by an exhilarating roller coaster drive over the towering sand dunes of Lahbab desert.

2. Sunset & Camp Welcome

Capture the magical desert sunset before heading to our traditional camp for a warm welcome with Arabic coffee, tea, and dates.

3. Camp Activities & Entertainment

Experience camel riding, henna tattoos, and relax in our lounge. Enjoy a series of live performances including Sufi, Fire, and Belly dance shows.

4. Gourmet Dinner & Return

Savor a delicious four-course gourmet barbeque meal beneath the starlit desert sky before a comfortable drive back to the city.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get the refund?

You can cancel your reservation up to 48 hours before your experience for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours will be subject to a 50% fee.

Can I change the travel date?

Modifications are allowed up to 48 hours before your scheduled experience. Please contact us to discuss any changes.

Do you offer insurance coverage?

We provide coverage for transportation, dune bashing incidents, and on-site camp events. For additional protection, we recommend personal travel insurance.
